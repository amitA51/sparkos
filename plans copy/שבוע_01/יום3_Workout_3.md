## תרגיל 1: Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown

**סטים:** 1 | **חזרות:** 8-10 | **סטי חימום:** 2-3 | **מנוחה:** 2-3 min

**עצימות:**
- Last Set RPE: ~6

**תרגילי חלופה:**
- Neutral-Grip Pull- Up
- Dual-Handle Lat Pulldown

**הערות:**
> Do these pulldowns with the handle more out in front of you, more like a cross between pullover and a pulldown. Focus on feeling your lats working more than the weight you're using.
