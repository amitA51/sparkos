## תרגיל 1: Lean-Back Lat Pulldown

**סטים:** 2 | **חזרות:** 10-12 | **סטי חימום:** 2-3 | **מנוחה:** 2-3 min

**עצימות:**
- Early Sets RPE: ~7-8
- Last Set RPE: ~7-8

**תרגילי חלופה:**
- Lean-Back Machine Pulldown
- Pull-Up

**הערות:**
> Initiate the pulldown with a straight up posture. As you pull the bar down, lean back by about 15-30° to get the mid-back more involved. Softly touch the bar to your chest on every rep and, even though you're leaning back, still control the weight!

## תרגיל 4: Cable Paused Shrug-In

**סטים:** 2 | **חזרות:** 12-15 | **סטי חימום:** 2-3 | **מנוחה:** 1-2 min

**עצימות:**
- Early Sets RPE: ~7
- Last Set RPE: ~8-9

**תרגילי חלופה:**
- Machine Shrug
- DB Shrug

**הערות:**
> Shrug up and in. Think about shrugging "up to your ears." 1-2 second pause in the squeeze (at the top) of each rep, then another 1-2 second pause in the stretch (at the bottom) of each rep.
